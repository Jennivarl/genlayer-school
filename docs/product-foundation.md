# Product Foundation

GenLayer School is a full-use community product for GenLayer learners, builders, and Gen-Fren participants.

## Product Pillars

- Academy: courses, lessons, labs, quizzes, and progress.
- Community: monthly ecosystem spotlights and builder stories.
- Weekly prep: GenLayer weekly summaries and Gen-Fren prep quizzes.
- Certification: off-chain eligibility first, on-chain certificates later.
- Developer bridge: practical Intelligent Contract examples and GenLayerJS integration.

## First Build Target

The first working version should make the public platform useful before account complexity:

- Browse learning tracks.
- Read lessons.
- Explore community spotlights.
- Read weekly Gen-Fren summaries.
- Take local prep quizzes.
- View certificate pathways.

Persistent accounts, Supabase, CMS publishing, and contract minting come after the public learning loop is stable.
